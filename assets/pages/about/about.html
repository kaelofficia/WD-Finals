ABOUT US

Our Story

Datablitz is a well-known retail chain in the Philippines specializing in video games, consoles, and related accessories. The company has become a staple in the Filipino gaming community, offering a wide range of gaming products and services

Over the years, Datablitz expanded its presence, opening multiple branches across the Philippines. This expansion helped it reach a wider audience and solidify its position as a leading gaming retailer in the country.

Values

Datablitz, a leading gaming retailer in the Philippines, is known for its commitment to customer satisfaction, integrity, quality, and community engagement. The company prioritizes excellent service through knowledgeable staff and ensures the authenticity of its products. Datablitz has built a strong reputation for reliability and sustainability. It continues actively supports the gaming community through events and activities, fostering lasting relationships with customers and partners

Mission

The mission of Datablitz centers around providing the best possible experience for gamers in the Philippines. It is dedicated to providing quality products and services to its customers. It focuses on delivering high-quality products, exceptional customer service, and fostering a vibrant gaming community

Vision

Datablitz offers a comprehensive range of gaming products, including the latest video games, popular gaming consoles, essential gaming accessories, PC hardware components, and gaming-related merchandise. They also provide budget-friendly pre-owned games and consoles, ensuring gamers have access to a wide variety of high-quality products for an optimal gaming experience

Products

Datablitz offers a wide range of products including the latest and classic video games, gaming consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch), essential gaming accessories (controllers, headsets, keyboards, mice), PC hardware components, and gaming merchandise. They also provide pre-owned games and consoles for budget-friendly options
